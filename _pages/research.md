My research lies at the intersection of law and politics. I study democratic backsliding, judicial institutions, and how people form attitudes toward courts, with particular interest in comparative and experimental approaches.

## Research interests

- Law and politics
- Democratic backsliding
- Judicial institutions and public attitudes toward courts
- Comparative politics and political methodology
- Experimental research designs

## Works in progress

My current projects examine these questions through comparative and experimental approaches. Project titles and descriptions will be added as the work develops.
